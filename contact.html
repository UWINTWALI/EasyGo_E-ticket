Customer Support FAQ

We're Here to Assist You

How do I book a ticket? ›

To book a ticket, visit our website and select your desired route, date, and time. Follow the prompts to complete the booking process. If you encounter any difficulties, our support team is available to guide you through the process.

What payment methods are accepted? ›

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and online banking. Our secure payment gateway ensures that your transactions are safe and convenient.

How can I cancel my ticket? ›

If you need to cancel your ticket, log in to your account on our website and navigate to the 'My Bookings' section. Follow the cancellation process, and our team will assist you with the refund procedure if applicable.
